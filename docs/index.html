Our Mission

We plan to take scripts from the 2010 show, Adventure Time, and transform them into readable xml

using REGEX and other technologies that we learned in class. After, we will analyze them for

specific character dialogue and form an analysis. Our Workflow tab will show our thought process

and drafting. The Analysis tab will cover our answer to our thesis question. A link to our Github is

also included
